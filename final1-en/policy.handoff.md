## 2. Role of the Deliverers (Researcher / Coordinator)

1. The deliverers acted solely as **researchers, functional design compilers, and coordinators**.
2. The deliverers are **not** the construction contractor, **not** the certifying structural engineer, and did **not** perform certified engineering calculations, welding work, foundation design, or installation work.
3. Any budget estimates or figures prepared by the deliverers are approximations **for budgeting purposes only** and do not constitute certification of structural strength or safety.

## 3. Role and Acknowledgement of the Recipient (Owner / Licensed Professional Engineer)

The Recipient acknowledges and agrees that:

1. The Recipient is the **Owner, Licensed Professional Engineer (PE licence), and Site Inspector/Supervisor** for this project.
2. The Recipient is the **party responsible for reviewing, verifying, computing/confirming, and certifying/approving the design** before construction, using their own **professional judgement**. The Recipient's signature of approval constitutes their own professional certification.
3. The Recipient is the **decision-maker for proceeding with construction and opening for use**, and is the party **who contracts and supervises the construction contractor**.
4. The Recipient acknowledges that the delivered items are preliminary (per §1) and accepts the duty to verify and certify as stated above.

---

## 4. Safety Recommendations (from the Deliverers)

The deliverers **strongly recommend** that the Recipient undertake the following for the safety of all users:

1. Verify and certify structural strength, foundation, and weld quality **before opening for use**
2. Verify concrete slab thickness and anchor embedment depth on site
3. **Before allowing children to use the equipment**, ensure the structure has been inspected/certified and that fall protection (e.g., EPDM rubber flooring) is installed as specified in the design
4. Monitor the 3 watch points identified in document 4/4 (monkey bar structure · pull-up bar span · cantilevered members)

The level and method of verification, as well as the approval decision, are the **professional judgement and responsibility of the Recipient** as the licensed engineer.
