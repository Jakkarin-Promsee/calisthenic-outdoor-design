## 1. Project in Brief

Outdoor calisthenics gym, rear garden · ~6 × 5 m plot · Users: 1 adult + 1 child ~5 years old. Hot-dip galvanised steel throughout. Four equipment pieces + rubber flooring:

- **Pull-up bar** (SHS 76 posts, OD 34 bar) · **Snake bar** (SHS 76 posts, OD 42 + OD 34 tube)
- **Dip bar** (OD 42 frame) · **Monkey + Flying bar**, 5 m long (SHS 50 posts, OD 34 rungs) · **EPDM rubber flooring**

---

## 2. Assessment Summary

Under design load **EN 16630:2015 (1,942 N/station, dynamics included)** and specified materials (wall 3.2 mm), the preliminary first-order assessment finds **all members pass — none yield.** Primary structural members (posts, foundations, anchors) have high margins (utilisation 0.2–0.5).

---

## 3. Points Requesting Your Engineering Input

Listed in order of importance (full details in [spec.summary §3](spec.summary.md)):

| Point                                              | Brief Issue                                                                                                                        |
| -------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1. Triangle Brace — Monkey Bar (Zone 4)**         | The 2.70 m post passes only because of this brace. Recommend confirming its weld integrity and never using the frame without it |
| **2. Pull-up Bar OD 34 · 1.5 m span**              | Moderate margin (util 0.71 at 1 user). Recommend fully welded end connections, and consider whether to limit this bar to one user at a time or increase the tube wall thickness |
| **3. Cantilevered Members (snake side grips / dip nub)** | Cantilever moment at the weld (snake side grip util 0.95). Recommend a full-perimeter weld or a reduced cantilever length                    |
| **4. Rubber Flooring — Fall Protection**           | Monkey bar fall height ~1.70 m exceeds the typical 1.0 m rating of 25 mm EPDM. Recommend increasing rubber thickness under Monkey/Pull-up to ≥ 50 mm |
| **5. Corrosion Protection + Weld Quality**         | Choosing between hot-dip galvanising (full dip) and cold zinc spray, and confirming weld quality at dynamic-load points             |

---

## 4. Within Your Scope of Decision

As the owner, licensed engineer, and site inspector:

1. Review/verify calculations and **certify the design** before construction
2. Confirm foundation: M12×120 HDG anchors in C20/25 concrete slab ≥ 140 mm thick (verify thickness and condition on site)
3. Oversee on-site workmanship (welds, anchor embedment depth) and accept the works before opening for use — **especially before children use the equipment**

> All proposed remedies are suggestions for your consideration. The level and method of verification, as well as the approval decision, are the professional judgement and responsibility of the licensed engineer.
